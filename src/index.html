Emergency Response & Safety

About Us

About G.I. & Associates Foundation

Our mission is simple: to help victims of domestic violence and human trafficking in every way possible. We start by removing the person or persons from their current situation of danger and abuse, getting them safely housed immediately, and from there our comprehensive process begins.

We provide medical care, counseling, and all the resources victims need from legal procedures and protection to long-term housing, vehicle assistance, and relocation to a safe place—whether local or long distance. We help men, women, and children regardless of gender, religious belief, or lifestyle.

"If you're a victim of domestic violence or human trafficking, we are here to help you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week."
