About Us

About G.I. & Associates Foundation

G.I. & Associates Foundation is dedicated to helping victims of domestic violence and human trafficking in every way possible. Our mission is simple: to provide comprehensive support that starts with immediate safety and continues through every step of recovery.

We help men, women, and children regardless of gender, religious belief, or lifestyle. Our process begins by removing victims from dangerous situations, getting them safely housed immediately, and providing all the resources they need from medical care and counseling to legal procedures, protection, and long-term assistance.

"If you're a victim of domestic violence or human trafficking, we are here to help you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. No matter your situation, we are here for you."
