Emergency Response & Safety Services


When Every Moment Counts


At G.I. & Associates Foundation, we understand that escaping domestic violence or human trafficking requires immediate action. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 to provide immediate safety, crisis intervention, and emergency housing when you need it most. We work quickly and discreetly to ensure your safety while providing the support and resources you need to begin your journey to freedom.



Our Emergency Response Process

  1. Immediate crisis assessment and safety planning
  2. Emergency housing and shelter coordination
  3. Medical care and healthcare support
  4. Connection to ongoing support services

Emergency Services Include

  • 24/7 crisis hotline support
  • Emergency safe housing and shelter
  • Medical care coordination
  • Crisis intervention and safety planning
  • Immediate protection and security measures
