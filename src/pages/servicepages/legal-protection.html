Legal & Protection Services


Your Rights Matter


Navigating the legal system can be overwhelming, especially when you're dealing with trauma. Our legal advocacy team provides comprehensive support to help you understand your rights and access the legal protection you need. We work with experienced attorneys and legal advocates who specialize in domestic violence and human trafficking cases to ensure you have the legal support needed to secure your safety and rights.



Our Legal Support Process

  1. Legal rights assessment and consultation
  2. Protection order filing and court proceedings
  3. Court accompaniment and advocacy
  4. Ongoing legal representation and support

Legal & Protection FAQs

Answers to common questions about our legal advocacy and protection services.

  • We provide protection orders, court advocacy, legal representation, rights education, and assistance with filing police reports. Our legal team specializes in domestic violence and human trafficking cases.

  • Our legal advocacy services are provided at no cost to victims. We believe that everyone deserves access to legal protection regardless of their financial situation.

  • Yes, our advocates can accompany you to court hearings and legal proceedings. We provide emotional support and help ensure your rights are protected throughout the process.

  • We guide you through the entire process of obtaining a protection order, from filing the paperwork to appearing in court. Our legal team ensures you understand each step and your rights.

  • Yes, we provide legal representation for both civil and criminal matters related to domestic violence and human trafficking. Our attorneys work to ensure justice is served and your rights are protected.
