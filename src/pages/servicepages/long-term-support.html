Long-term Support & Recovery


Rebuilding Your Life


Recovery from domestic violence and human trafficking is a journey, not a destination. Our long-term support services are designed to help you rebuild your life with dignity, independence, and hope for the future. We provide comprehensive support including counseling, long-term housing assistance, vehicle support, and relocation services to help you not just survive, but thrive in your new life free from violence and exploitation.



Our Recovery Support Process

  1. Comprehensive needs assessment and recovery planning
  2. Counseling and therapy services coordination
  3. Long-term housing and financial assistance
  4. Ongoing support and community connection

Long-term Services Include

  • Professional counseling and therapy
  • Long-term housing assistance and support
  • Vehicle assistance and transportation
  • Relocation services and fresh start support
  • Employment and financial assistance programs

FAQ's

Long-term Support FAQs

Answers to common questions about our long-term support and recovery services.

  • Our long-term support services are available for as long as you need them. Recovery timelines vary for each person, and we're committed to supporting you throughout your entire healing journey.

  • We provide individual therapy, group counseling, trauma-informed care, and specialized support for domestic violence and human trafficking survivors. All counseling is provided by licensed professionals.

  • Yes, we provide relocation assistance both locally and long-distance. We help with moving, finding new housing, connecting with local resources, and establishing yourself in your new community.

  • Our long-term support services are provided at no cost to survivors. We believe that everyone deserves access to comprehensive support as they rebuild their lives.

  • Yes, we offer job training, resume building, and employment assistance to help you gain financial independence and build a stable career in your new life.
