Comprehensive Support

Our 7-Step Plan of Action for Victims

We follow a comprehensive, step-by-step process to ensure every victim receives the support they need to escape danger, heal, and rebuild their lives. Our approach addresses immediate safety needs while providing long-term recovery support.

1

Remove from Unsafe Situation

We immediately remove victims from their current unsafe situation and get them housed in safe emergency housing. Our priority is ensuring their immediate safety and security.

  • 24/7 emergency response
  • Immediate safety assessment
  • Secure transportation to safe location
  • Emergency housing placement
2

Medical Treatment

We ensure victims receive any necessary medical treatment, including emergency care, physical examinations, and mental health assessments.

  • Emergency medical care
  • Physical health assessments
  • Mental health evaluations
  • Ongoing medical support
3

Legal Protection

We help victims obtain any legal paperwork that may be needed, such as restraining orders, personal protection orders, and other legal protections.

  • Restraining orders
  • Personal protection orders
  • Legal advocacy
  • Court accompaniment
4

Comprehensive Intake

We conduct a thorough intake process to assess needs such as reuniting with family, housing requirements, medical information, and other essential needs.

  • Family reunification planning
  • Housing needs assessment
  • Medical history documentation
  • Personal goal setting
5

Counseling Support

We provide any counseling needs, including individual therapy, group support, trauma counseling, and specialized mental health services.

  • Individual therapy sessions
  • Group support meetings
  • Trauma-informed counseling
  • Family counseling services
6

Safe Relocation Process

We begin the process of safe relocation, whether it be local or long distance, ensuring victims can start fresh in a secure environment.

  • Relocation planning
  • Safe housing arrangements
  • Transportation assistance
  • Community integration support
7

New Life Funding

We begin the funding process for all things needed to start their new life, including housing, vehicle, child care, resume building, and job leads.

  • Housing assistance
  • Vehicle procurement
  • Child care support
  • Job placement assistance
  • Resume and interview preparation
